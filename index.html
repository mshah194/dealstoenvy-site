One-time setup

  1. Upload the spreadsheet template to Google Sheets.
  2. Publish the Deals_Export tab as CSV.
  3. Paste the CSV URL into assets/js/sheet-config.js.
  4. Upload the updated site files to your host.

After that, daily posting happens only inside your sheet.
